# Prompts para Logo ZapLivre - Grok Image Generation

## 🎨 PROMPT PRINCIPAL (Recomendado)

```
Create a modern, minimalist logo for "ZapLivre", a Brazilian P2P messaging app for businesses. 

Design requirements:
- Style: Clean, geometric, tech-forward
- Icon: A speech bubble with an arrow (→) integrated, symbolizing "passing a message"
- Colors: Vibrant cyan (#00D9FF) and electric purple (#7C3AED) gradient
- Typography: Bold, modern sans-serif font for "ZapLivre"
- Layout: Icon on the left, wordmark on the right
- Must work in monochrome (black/white)
- Must be scalable (app icon to billboard)

The logo should feel: fast, trustworthy, Brazilian, modern, professional yet friendly.

Vector style, flat design, no shadows, white background.
```

"Remove [elemento X] and emphasize [elemento Y]"

Quanto mais específico, melhor:
- ✅ "Speech bubble with arrow integrated"

- ✅ "Cyan #00D9FF to purple #7C3AED gradient"
- ❌ "Cool colors"
